Graduation is an important milestone for kindergarten and preschool students. Little graduates look forward to the big day where they can put on a cap and gown just like the big kids wear. Proud parents will take photos to share with family and friends. Kindergarten teachers, daycare center coordinators, and pre school teachers can recognize achievements as they send students toward the next grade.

Jostens Kinderkraft has everything to make graduation day special. Find caps, gowns, certificates and diplomas, sashes, tassels, and more with sizes for kinder graduation to elementary school graduation. With easy online ordering and packages for every budget, we've got graduation covered.

Many schools include graduation items in the cost of tuition or they provide graduation items to children. Some schools are using a different approach and asking parents to order. We provide free materials to schools to send home to parents with ordering instructions.

Parents can also order preschool, daycare, or kindergarten graduation items online from our website. Start shopping now!
