How do I measure for gowns?
Measure from the top of their heads to the floor. The gowns come in 7 sizes (3'4", 3'8", 4'0", 4'4", 4'8", 5'0", 5'1") and should be rounded up from the child’s height to the next size.
Do the caps come with tassels?
All of our Grad Packages conveniently include both a cap and tassel. Á la carte caps do not include a tassel, but one can be purchased seperately.
How do I wear my cap?
The graduation cap is to be worn flat on your head.
How do I care for my gown?
Upon receipt of your graduation gown, promptly remove it from the packaging and hang it up. To remove wrinkles, you may choose to press with a cool iron, or hang gown in a steamy room. Safety stitching at all stress points and a hidden zipper mean no raw edges inside or out.
Why do I see options for two colors on the order form?
The printable order form allows the school to select two gown colors if they choose to have boys and girls wear different colors. If all graduates are wearing the same color, disregard the option for the second color.
How quickly will I receive my order?
Orders will be received within 3 weeks from order. If you need orders expedited, we offer an express delivery for an additional charge of $10.00, provided all products are available.
I have placed an order as a school. How can parents order?
Parents can also order online. Jostens will provide schools with free materials to send home with parents that instruct them where to order and what selections of colors the school has made. Parents can order online or fill out the form and send payment back to the school so the school can place a large order.
How does the create-a-cap work?
The Create-A-Cap is a fun art project for students to complete months before graduation to get children excited. It is also a way for schools to get older students involved in graduation. Create-A-Caps are packaged in sets of 10. Tassels not included. Students can use markers and crayons to decorate the hat and then assemble it to complete the craft project.
